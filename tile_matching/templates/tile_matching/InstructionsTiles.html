Instructions

This is a simple matching game. You can select any two tiles at a time.
If they match, they will be turned over permanently. If they don't, they will be turned face down again and you can try another pair

The less turns you take to turn over all of the tiles, the more you earn

The image below is an example of a game in progress. Above the grid, is the current star rating base on the number of moves, the number of moves, and the number of tiles matched
