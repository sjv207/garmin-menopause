Now Stage 1 of the study (Fixed payout) takes place, in which you receive {{C.FIXED_RATE}} ECU. You have 2 minutes for this task.

As in the previous stages, you have 2 minutes to correctly solve as many tile games as possible. Your payout for this stage will depend on your performance relative to the performance of a group of participants.

Division into groups: Your are now randomly assigned to a group of 4.

You will not find out who the other participants in your group are either during or after the experiment. So, all decisions will remain anonymous.

Payoff: If Stage 3 is the stage selected for payout, then your payoff will depend on how many tile games you have correctly solved in this stage compared to the other 3 participants in your group.

The TWO participants who have the largest number of correct matrices are the winners of the competition. Each of them receives {{C.COMPETITION_RATE}} ECU for each correct matrix, while the other TWO participants in the group receive no payout. The winning participants are determined randomly if there is a tie between two participants.

When you are ready, please click on "Next" and Stage 3 will start automatically.
