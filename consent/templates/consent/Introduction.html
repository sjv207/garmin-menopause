This study has two parts:

Part 1 - Three stages involving a real-effort task
Part 2 - A short survey

In Part 1, you will complete the same real-effort task three times. What changes across the stages is how you are paid for your performance in the task. Before each stage, you will receive clear instructions explaining the specific payment rules.

Your final payment will depend on your own performance and, in some stages, on the performance of other participants. After completing all three stages, one stage will be randomly selected to determine your bonus payment. This bonus will be paid in addition to a participation (show-up) fee of Â£3.00.

All decisions are made anonymously, and all responses will remain strictly confidential.

At the end of the study, you will be informed about your total earnings and the outcome of the selected stage.

Detailed instructions for each stage will be displayed on screen before it begins.
