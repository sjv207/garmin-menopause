During the study, all participants will be asked to complete a task. Both your own decisions and those of the other participants may determine your payout according to the rules explained on the next pages.

Each participant makes their decisions anonymously, and all answers remain confidential.

The currency in the experiment is the Experimental Currency Unit (ECU).

The experiment consists of 4 Stages and a short survey. You will be informed of the outcome of the different stages at the end of the experiment. One of the stages is selected at random for your payout of any bonus earned at the rate of {{conversion_rate}} ECU = {{ecus}}.

The 4 stages are:

  • Stage 1: Fixed fee - you will perform the task and recieve a fixed fee, regardless of performance
  • Stage 2: Piece rate - you will be paid a fixed amount for each correct response. The more you solve, the more you get paid
  • Stage 3: Competitive - you will be paid based on your performance relative to other participants. If you perform better than others, you will earn more
  • Stage 4: Choice - you will have the option to choose which of the previous 3 stages again.

Your total earnings from the experiment are the sum of your payments for the randomly selected stage (bonus) plus a participation fee of {{session.config.participation_fee}}.

The instructions for the respective stages are displayed at the start of each of the stages.
